Trauma Impact Assessment by Jahnavi Shukla

Why We Are Doing This

This assessment is designed to help individuals understand how past traumatic events may be affecting their daily lives. By answering these questions, you can gain insights into your emotional and psychological well-being.

How It Works

You will answer 20 questions about your experiences and feelings. Based on your responses, we will generate a report that assesses the impact of trauma on your life. This report can be shared with a mental health professional for further guidance.
